FM Morena 100.5 — Catamarca

As Creative Strategist for FM Morena, I led a complete brand modernization—moving the station from local FM mainstay to a multi‑platform audio brand with a global footprint.

scope of work

  • Re‑designed visual identity and on‑air graphics; launched digital video programming.
  • Developed FM Morena mobile app (5,000+ installs to date) and placed the station on major streaming directories (Streema, Radio Garden, TuneIn).
  • Integrated audience analytics and performance advertising to expand reach across provincial and expatriate communities.
  • Introduced podcast and mobile‑first show formats to improve shareability on social and messaging apps.
  • Established a coherent social presence on Facebook and Instagram, standardising tone and visual language.

measurable results

  • Listener base grew from an estimated 9 k local listeners to more than 40 k global weekly streams within 12 months.
  • Facebook community surpassed 10 k followers with engagement up 180% year‑over‑year.
  • Advertiser inquiries doubled, adding new segments in retail, tourism, and education.
  • Video snippets averaged 25 k views per release across social platforms.
