Rasta Alfajor - Argentina

As Creative Strategist for Rasta Alfajores

I led the brand's first marketing campaign, positioning it as a standout product in kiosks across Argentina. The campaign was notable for its use of AI-driven workflows, ensuring efficiency and creativity in the production process.

Scope of Work

- Reimagined the brand with a vibrant identity that captured the spirit of Argentine tradition while appealing to modern sensibilities.
- Created the first TV and mobile spot using AI tools for everything from storyboard generation to visual effects, enabling rapid iteration and cost-effective production.
- Leveraged AI-based video editing software to streamline post-production, enhancing the spot’s overall quality and visual appeal.
- Implemented AI-driven data analysis to refine social media targeting and maximize engagement with the local audience.

Measurable Results

- The AI-powered campaign reached 2M+ views in just two weeks, with highly targeted social media content.
- Instagram followers grew by 300% in the first month, with engagement boosted by AI-enhanced content recommendations.
- Sales saw a significant uptick across kiosks nationwide, reflecting the campaign's ability to connect with consumers effectively.
